Split complex bills — uneven shares, fractions & reconciled totals.

Bill

Each cell is a person's share of an item. The ×/÷ toggle on each row sets how: × multiply — everyone pays weight × price (quantities); ÷ divide — everyone splits the price, weight ÷ total × price (a greyed /n shows the total). Hover a row and tap = to split it evenly. Set who paid with the chip in the Paid column (a section's payer sets the default; any item can override) and the owing matrix shows who owes whom. Reconcile a section to scale everyone to the amount actually paid (a discount, tax, or tip). Everything saves in this browser. Type set in Inter.
