Split complex bills — uneven shares, fractions & reconciled totals.

Bill

Each cell is the share of that item's price a person pays — cost = price × cell. Enter a fraction (1/4 each for a four-way split), a quantity (2), a decimal, or leave blank. Hover a row and tap ÷ to split it evenly. Set who paid with the chip in the Paid column (a section's payer sets the default; any item can override) and the owing matrix shows who owes whom. Reconcile a section to scale everyone to the amount actually paid (a discount, tax, or tip). Everything saves in this browser. Type set in Inter.
